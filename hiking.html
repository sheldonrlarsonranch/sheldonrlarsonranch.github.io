Hiking

There are miles and miles of hiking trails throughout the gorgeous Wasatch Mountain Range. Whether it's to an arch, waterfall, or jaw-dropping panoramic views, there's a hike here for you! From easy to strenous and short to long, we've listed our top recommended hikes in the area! Read more about our recommended hikes below, or if you want more hikes, we recommend also looking at all trails or hiking project.

Maple Canyon Loop Trail

2.7 miles 1,299 feet ■ Moderate

Clear out your phone storage for this hike because I guarantee you'll fill it up with pictures! This hike has it all, an arch, 360° panoramic views, and a cave. This hike is a must do!

Box Canyon Trail

1.2 miles 462 feet ■ Moderate

A unique cobblestone slot canyon with a waterfall and a few boulders to scramble over make this hike unbelievably fun! This is a great short hike to combine with the Maple Canyon Loop Trail.

South Tent Mtn Summit

4.2 miles 1,059 feet ■ Moderate

South Tent Mtn is the high point in Sanpete county and makes for a gorgeous hike with views of the entire valley! You can turn this into a 7 mile loop with North Tent Mtn or into an even shorter 2 mile hike!

Mount Nebo

8.8 miles 5,728 feet ◆ Difficult

Mt Nebo is the highest peak in the Wasatch Range at a staggering 11,929 feet! There are several trails to the summit (this one likely being the most difficult, but also the most beautiful and our favorite)!

Milky Falls Trail

0.2 miles 36 feet ● Easy

This is a very easy hike with a gorgeous cascading waterfall in some of the prettiest parts of the mountain. I recommend combining this hike with several ATV or mountain biking trails in the area.
