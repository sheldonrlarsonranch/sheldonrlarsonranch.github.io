Country Cabins on Sheldon R. Larson Ranch

We have two beautiful country-style cabins available for rent located three miles west of Ephraim, Utah. Our cabins are situated minutes from the world famous Arapeen trail system with some of the best ATV and snowmobile routes this side of Florida! You'll also find Maple Canyon, renowned in the rock climbing and hiking community for it's unique cobblestone cliffs, only miles away. We aim to ensure that our cabins provide you with everything you need including all the essentials and amenities of your own home so that you feel at home the minute you walk through the door. So no matter what you do, we know you won't be disappointed with our country-style cabins. Feel free to contact us with any questions or even if you're just looking for recommendations for the area. We are always happy to help!
