Area Attractions

The beautiful Sanpete valley has so much to offer, from the world famous Arapeen trail system to a long, rich history! Here we've compiled a short list of our favorite activities. But really, you can't go wrong with any of these activites, so follow the links to learn more about what this area has to offer and get started on your next vacation!

ATV

Being minutes from the world famous Arapeen trail system, you have over 350,000 acres of interconnecting, beautiful mountain trails. So from your motorcycle to side-by-side, bring your ATV an enjoy the pristine landscape Sanpete Valley has to offer.

Hiking

With the Manti-Lasal Mountain Range next door, you have some of the best hiking trails in the Rocky Mountains! From waterfalls and panoramic views to deep forest canopies, there are plenty of hiking trails for all ages and skill levels!

Rock Climbing

The cobblestone climbing of Maple Canyon is just minutes away from our cabins. Climbers from around the world come to test their skills on Maple Canyon’s unique cobblestone cliffs. With hundreds of climbs, it's easy to find a climb for everyone from novice to expert!

Snowmobiling

Countless miles of untouched powder and bowls that offer some of the best backcountry mountain sledding in the state. SnoWest magazine wrote, "Boondocker’s Heaven- If there was ever a place on earth that could lay claim to that title, it would have to be Utah’s Sanpete County."

Mountain Biking

Have you ever rode a mountain bike over 50 miles at an altitude of nearly 10,000 feet? Skyline Drive has breathtaking views that will Expand your outlook on life. This area offers many single or multi-day adventures for you to enjoy on your bike.

History

Sanpete’s Heritage is prevalent today with many buildings built in the 1800’s. Native American sites, pioneer homesteads, historic buildings, and cultural sites draw history and culture buffs to this area. Forbes listed neighboring town Spring City, UT as one of the top prettiest towns in America.

And So Much More

From state parks to boating and snowkiting, Sanpete valley is an outdoor paradise! There is something here for everyone, including a full scale replica of a nearly fully intact Columbian mammoth that was unearthed here in 1988, art museums, and many more family-fun activities!
