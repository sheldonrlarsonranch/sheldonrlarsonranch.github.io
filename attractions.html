Area Attractions

The beautiful Sanpete valley has much to offer, from the world famous Arapeen trail system to its long, rich history! We've compiled a short list of our favorite activities below to get you started on planning your trip. But really, you can't go wrong with any of these activities! Learn more about what the gorgeous Sanpete valley has to offer and get started on your next vacation with us!

ATV

Being minutes from the world famous Arapeen trail system, you have over 350,000 acres of interconnecting, beautiful mountain trails. From a motorcycle to a side-by-side, bring your ATV and enjoy the endless miles of pristine landscape Sanpete Valley has to offer.

Hiking

With the Manti-Lasal Mountain Range next door, you have some of the best hiking trails in the Rocky Mountains! From waterfalls and panoramic views to deep forest canopies, there are plenty of hiking trails for all ages and skill levels!

Rock Climbing

The renowned climbing of Maple Canyon is just minutes away from our cabins! Climbers from around the world come to test their skills on Maple Canyon’s unique cobblestone cliffs. With hundreds of climbs, it's easy to find a climb for everyone from novice to Alex Honnold!

Snowmobiling

"Boondocker’s Heaven- If there was ever a place on earth that could lay claim to that title, it would have to be Utah’s Sanpete County" -SnoWest magazine. Enjoy countless miles of untouched powder and bowls that offer some of the best backcountry snowmobiling in the country!

Mountain Biking

Have you ever rode a mountain bike over 50 miles at an altitude of nearly 10,000 feet? Skyline Drive has breathtaking views that will expand your outlook on life. Sanpete County offers many single or multi-day adventures for you to enjoy on your bike.

History

Sanpete’s Heritage is prevalent today with many buildings built in the 1800s. Native American sites, historic buildings, and cultural sites draw history and culture buffs to this area. Spring City (listed as one of the prettiest towns in America by Forbes) is a must see for its history!

And So Much More

From state parks to boating and snowkiting, Sanpete valley is a vacation paradise! There is something here for everyone, including a nearly fully intact Columbian mammoth that was unearthed here in 1988, aquatic centers, and old-fashioned drive-in movies with burgers and fries.
