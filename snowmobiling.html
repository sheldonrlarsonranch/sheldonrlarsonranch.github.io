Snowmobiling

"Just once we'd like to ride in an area officially called Boondocker's Heaven. We're talking a place where, when you log on to www.topozone.com and type in Boondocker's Heaven, a specific spot in the snowbelt pops up. We thought we'd ridden in places that can easily be named that - that is, until we did some serious riding in central Utah. Official or not, that is Boondocker's Heaven." -SnoWest Magazine

Enjoy over 350,000 acres of the greatest snow on Earth. When riding on Skyline Drive in the Wasatch Monjtain Range you'll feel like you're on top of the world with views that go on as far as the eye can see. And as spectacular as these views are, the snowmobiling is even better. Read about some of our favorite places to go snowmobiling below, or for other suggesetions we recommend also looking at this free digital map or looking at Sanpete.com.

Spring City

Spring City has to be one of the prettiest small towns in the country. And it's not just us that think so! Forbes listed it as one of the top prettiest towns in America! In addition, only this town and Williamsburg, VA are listed as complete towns on the national history registry!
